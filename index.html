Don't Wordle

The game opposite to Wordle, Avoid guessing the word within 6 attempts.

What is Don 't Wordle

Wordle challenges players to guess a specific 5-letter word within 6 tries or less. Conversely, in Don 't Wordle, the objective is to avoid guessing the word altogether.

How to play Don 't Wordle

Avoid guessing the Wordle in 6 tries. Gather clues from your previous guesses. Click the question mark at the top right for detailed instructions.

UNDOs in Don 't Wordle

At the top, you 'll see the count of remaining valid words, which decreases with each guess. Should it dwindle too low, you have the option to use one of your 'UNDOs 'to backtrack.
